💡 Recognition Tips

  • Keep moving: ASL signs are dynamic movements - don't hold static positions
  • Repeat the signs: Perform each sign multiple times for better recognition
  • Follow the guide: Study the reference videos/images below to learn proper form
  • Good lighting: Ensure your hands are well-lit and clearly visible
  • Center your hands: Keep your signing space within the camera frame
